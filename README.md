# BooOS - SCARY STUFF PROCEED WITH CAUTION
### Back Story: May 10, 2024... At Apple CA, an Eric was ready to push something to production, and guess what, it was Friday... The macOS production build failed, and he lost his weekend, his daughter was in the hospital in New York with cancer, and he wanted to visit her this weekend. That weekend, he worked day and night... The cancer too! His daughter passed away. He wasn't informed, LOG: "fatal: eric@apple.com not found" on 14th of May, Monday... AND THAT MACOS BUILD REMAINED POSSESSED! BooOS!
(the story was completely made up by me...)
